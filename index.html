The gap we close

Wanting to shop Black-owned is easy.
Knowing who to trust is hard.

Directories list everyone, and you still have no idea who's good. Deal apps chase strangers in the door with the deepest discount. Woven does the one thing neither does: it sets a standard. Every brand is vetted, and worth coming back to. Think of us as the Michelin Guide for Black-owned brands, not the biggest list, the most trusted one.

The Standard

One mark. Earned, never bought.

This is the seal a Woven Certified brand earns and displays publicly, so a shopper knows at a glance that it has been vetted. Every brand is measured against the same four things, and can lose the mark if it stops meeting them.

01

Product quality & craft

The work holds up. We experience products firsthand and vet services through references.

02

Brand story & founder

A real founder, a real story. The people behind the brand are who they say they are.

03

Values & community impact

How the business shows up for the community it draws from, not just what it sells.

04

Customer experience

Ready for members to walk in the door: dependable, welcoming, able to deliver.

Woven is the guardian of the standard, not the owner of any brand. We are a caretaker of the trust between a brand and its community.
