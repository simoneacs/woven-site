The gap we close

Wanting to shop Black-owned is easy. Knowing who to trust is hard.

Directories list everyone. Deal apps chase strangers with the deepest discount. Neither tells you which brands are genuinely excellent, the ones worth coming back to.

Woven is built to close that gap. Think of us as the Michelin Guide for Black-owned brands: not the biggest list, the most trusted one.

A directory tells you who exists. A standard tells you who's worth it.

The Standard

One mark. Earned, never bought.

This is the seal a Woven Certified brand earns and displays publicly, so a shopper knows at a glance that it's been vetted. Every brand is measured against the same four pillars. A brand that clears the bar carries the mark. A brand that stops meeting the bar can lose it, and that's what makes it mean something.

01

Product quality & craft

The work holds up. We experience products firsthand and vet services through materials and references.

02

Brand story & founder

A real founder, a real story. The people behind the brand are who they say they are.

03

Values & community impact

How the business shows up for the community it draws from, not just what it sells.

04

Customer experience & readiness

Ready for members to walk in the door: dependable, welcoming, and able to deliver.

Woven is the guardian of the standard, not the owner of any brand. We're a caretaker of the trust between a brand and its community.
